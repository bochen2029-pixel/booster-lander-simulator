## 4. The MPPI frontier (the detailed situation — read before tuning)

**Architecture (works, don't change):** lateral-only HIER. Hoverslam owns throttle/ignition (in
execution AND in each rollout via a lean vertical proxy); MPPI owns only `a_lat` (2 world-lateral
channels). Rollout = `control_step`+`dynamics_deriv` at RK4 25 ms, warm-started on the hoverslam
sqrt-decel+velocity-lead `a_lat`, K=256, OU σ=1.5, replan @10 Hz. Cost is NORMALIZED (softmax healthy):
converging-velocity tracking + asymmetric outward penalty + **ZEM terminal** `T_ZEM·|r_xy+v_xy·t_go|²`
(foresight past the 5 s horizon) + strong touchdown `TD_RXY/TD_VXY` + bounded/clipped crash + adaptive λ.
Determinism: Philox lane=rollout, fixed pairwise-tree reduction (bit-stable under OpenMP). **Verified
deterministic** (run twice → identical).

**Why it doesn't land yet — TWO coupled problems:**

1. **Divert-reach vs soft-arrest trade-off (tuning, the immediate blocker).** MPPI diverts well
   (843 m→88 m) but the batch is ~59/60 **off-pad** with the conservative warm-start (`A_DECEL=1.3,
   VLAT=30`) — under-diverts. Raising it (`2.2/48`) reaches ~50-100 m but the a_lat *tilt near the
   ground* compromises the vertical arrest → **hard touchdown (td_v 8-11 → CRASHED even on-pad)**.
   Lowering the fade altitude (`h/400`→`h/150`) made it *worse* (more low tilt → 11 m/s). The real fix
   (untried/unconverged): make MPPI arrive **CENTERED (r≈0 AND v_xy≈0) by ~400 m** so the fade-to-
   vertical leaves no residual — i.e. front-load the divert + null v_xy *earlier* and harder (stronger
   running/terminal v_xy weight relative to the reach), not divert harder into the dead zone. Current
   file left at MPPI-2's tuned baseline (`A_DECEL=1.3, VLAT=30, fade=h/400`); a big comment block near
   the top of `guidance_mppi.c` records this.

2. **Physics divert ceiling (may cap the rate regardless).** MPPI-2 measured (and I confirmed) the
   aero-divert ceiling from 12 km is ~**400-500 m** even with the flat 15° cap. AERO_OFFSET is **mean
   500 σ150**, so a large fraction of seeds start beyond the ceiling and are **genuinely unlandable by
   any controller**. P4's old ~860 m estimate was optimistic. This is the same situation that caused
   D-006's 800→500 retune — **500 is still too high for the real ceiling.**

**Key knobs in `guidance_mppi.c`:** `A_DECEL`, `VLAT_MAX`, `T_LEAD` (warm-start divert); `T_ZEM`,
`TD_VXY`, `TD_RXY`, `Q_VXYERR`, `Q_VOUT` (cost); `SIG_ALAT`, `MPPI_K`, `MPPI_REPLAN_DECIM`; the two
`s=h_feet/400` fade lines (cmd_from_u_lean + mppi_execute).

## 5. Roadmap — recommended paths forward (ranked; optimal first)

### ★ OPTIMAL PATH (fastest to a real AERO landing rate)
**A. Resolve the divert-vs-arrest tuning AND right-size the scenario to the ceiling — in parallel.**
   1. **Measure the true ceiling** with a compiled-C study (house pattern, `runs/sandbox/*.c`): for a
      grid of initial offsets, find the max offset that lands with an *oracle* bang-bang divert (no lag).
      This tells you the real landable-offset cap → whether mean-500 is well-posed.
   2. **Retune AERO_OFFSET dispersion to ~mean 300-350 σ100** (an ADR, exactly like D-006's 800→500 —
      match the scenario to the measured ceiling). This makes ≥90% *physically achievable* and is the
      honest fix; keep a separate `aero_far`/`chaos` scenario for the stretch cases.
   3. **Tune MPPI for "centered by 400 m":** raise `T_ZEM`/`Q_VXYERR` and the *running* v_xy weight so
      MPPI nulls cross-range velocity EARLY (front-loaded), keep `A_DECEL`~1.5-1.8 (moderate reach), and
      keep the fade at ~h/400. Validate on the 16 `regression_worstcase.json` ICs + a 300-seed batch.
   Expected: AERO_OFFSET (retuned) ≥90% under MPPI. **This is the highest-value next move.**

## 8. Hard-won lessons (don't re-derive — full detail in DECISIONS.md)
- **Lateral-only MPPI** — never let MPPI touch throttle; the hoverslam vertical burn is proven (97%),
  MPPI's edge is ONLY the cross-range null. (Both subagents independently reached this.)
- **Warm-start MPPI on the working reactive controller** — search corrections, don't discover from
  noise. This single change unlocked the softmax (ESS 1→healthy).
- **Cost must be NORMALIZED + crash BOUNDED** — raw canon weights (40|r|², crash=1e6) rail λ / collapse ESS.
- **The retropropulsive burn is SRP-drag-shielded** → the suicide-burn feasibility shoot must be
  THRUST-ONLY (crediting drag ignites too late → slam-in).
- **Sim STRUCT is qbar>80 kPa, NOT an AoA side-load** → 15° AoA is safe (aero-descent peaks ~36 kPa).
- **Banking the high-thrust entry burn needs the `a_vert_ref` fix** (else a_lat→tilt over-produces
  lateral ~3.4× → 17 km off) AND a null-out reversal (P5 §5.1) — naive constant bank is catastrophic.
- **Instrument the plant before tuning the controller** (the `vrad` column; 5× vindicated).
- **AERO_OFFSET mean-500 likely exceeds the real ~400-500 m divert ceiling** — right-size it (ADR).
